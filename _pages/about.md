## Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in Psychological Science and Human Development at Cornell University, working under the supervision of Professor Robert J. Sternberg. I also hold a Ph.D. in psychology with a specialization in industrial–organizational psychology. My scholarship focuses on wisdom and leadership, including how wisdom develops and how it can be cultivated in individuals and organizations.


## Research Interests
1. Wisdom and its development
2. Leadership
3. Cultural intelligence and attitudinal intelligence
4. Motivation and human needs
5. Talent management and education


Research Approach
------
I use a mix of theory-building and empirical methods (e.g., qualitative research, quantitative research, and applied organizational research) to understand how people and leaders make decisions in complex real-world contexts—and how we can design interventions that support wise and ethical action.

--- layout: archive title: "Research Projects" permalink: /portfolio/ author_profile: true --- {% include base_path %} {% for post in site.portfolio %} {% include archive-single.html %} {% endfor %} My research asks a simple question: **How do people develop and deploy their potential—and when does that potential translate into positive change?** Across projects, I study three connected pieces of this puzzle: 1. **Ability vs. attitude:** People may have strong abilities but differ in whether and how they deploy them across person–task–situation contexts. 2. **Human needs & motivation:** Needs and motivational processes help explain *when* people choose to use their abilities—and what goals they pursue. 3. **Wisdom & wise leadership:** Not all ability is used for positive ends. Wisdom and wise leadership clarify how people can direct their abilities toward positive outcomes.
